# Failure Labs

## Purpose

Failure labs teach diagnosis by intentionally placing learners in controlled broken states. They are curriculum designs only and must be converted into safe labs later.

## Safety Rules

Failure labs must avoid destructive production actions, real secrets, real customer data, and paid resources unless cleanup and cost warnings are explicit.
